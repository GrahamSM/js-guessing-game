How to Play

Input a number between 1 and 10 as your guess, and a bet value between $5 and $10

Your guess will be compared to a randomly generated number

If you guess the right number, your total earnings will increase by the value of your bet

If you guess the wrong number, your earnings decrease by the value of your bet

HOWEVER, your guess is within 1 of the actual number, nothing happens

You can play as many times as you like until you wish to exit, or you run out of money
